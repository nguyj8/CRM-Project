Tools and Resources

We're in this together, and we'll do everything we can to help make sure you're ready for whatever happens.

Meet mySHIELD® A personalized online destination for risk management resources to help support your business.

Training Today This learning management system provides online education programs for employees and supervisors on 200+ training topics. More than 800 businesses — and counting — have taken advantage of these tools.

Federated Employment Practices Network® (FEPN) FEPN provides access to independent resources that provide best practices and counsel on many human resources and employment law issues. FEPN includes resources such as a handbook builder, sample employment policies, supervisor and employee online training modules, webinars, podcasts, and a job description builder.
Federated Insurance clients who carry Employment-Related Practices Liability (ERPL) coverage have access to a network of independent employment law attorneys. These independent attorneys provide quick, confidential, documented responses to your state- and business-specific questions on a full range of employment law topics.

Seven Minute Safety Trainer (SMST) Safety on the go! This mobile app helps our clients hold “toolbox talk” safety meetings remotely with more than 300 training topics.

J.J. Keller and Associates Inc.® Video on Demand This video-on-demand service helps clients increase safety awareness, identify practices and obtain general information on changing regulations. Video libraries cover such topics as construction, driving, hazardous materials, human resources, and workplace safety.
